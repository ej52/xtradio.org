XTRadio

We focus on electronic music, especially genres like deephouse, glitchop, house, trap, future bass, nudisco, ghettofunk, future house, etc..

Our main focus are independent artists, djs, producers who release their music on soundcloud or bandcamp. You can get in touch on facebook, soundcloud, mixcloud or just email us.

Feel free to send us your music, podcast, mix, we're always open to air new things. This project is non profit, we do it purely for the love of music.
